About Jupi

Jupi is a curious and friendly astronaut from the swirling storms of Jupiter. With a heart full of joy and a backpack full of stardust, Jupi travels the galaxy in a tiny spaceship, making new friends and sharing the wonders of the cosmos. Jupi believes that every planet, star, and creature has a story to tell, and is on a mission to hear them all!
